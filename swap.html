What is Δzyr Swap?

Featuring a simple interface and user friendly design, familiar operation as on EVM.

The platform enables the swapping of tokens available in the Pool Activity.

Users provide liquidity in BRC20 with BTC, receiving 100% of transaction fees from swap activities as rewards.

Future features include advanced pro trade options such as limit orders, price alerts, fundamental project information in the price chart, holder and whale analysis, etc.
